Our Story

Your Neighborhood Nail Spot

Sophia's Nail Salon has been a staple of the Atlanta community — a place where everyone is welcome, prices are fair, and the work is done right.

We're open late every weeknight until 10PM, so you can always fit in an appointment after work or school. No rushing, no pressure.

We also have booths available for licensed nail technicians looking for a space to grow their clientele in a supportive environment.
